About Marc

My digital artwork focuses on portraits and immersive studies, using warm colors to bring characters and stories to life.

Hi, I’m Marc Louise, a digital artist based in Sto. Niño Norte, Arevalo, Iloilo City, Philippines. I specialize in digital sketches, paintings, and character designs. My work explores warm color palettes, textures, and intimate storytelling, aiming to bring emotions and moments to life through my art. I create my artwork using Ibis Paint and PicsArt for editing, and I’m always exploring new techniques to refine my style. Commissions and collaborations are welcome, both locally and remotely.

  • Specialties: digital painting, character design, color studies
  • Tools: Ibis Paint, PicsArt, FlipaClip
  • Available for commissions & collaborations
